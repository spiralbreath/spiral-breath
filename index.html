✦ What Spiral Breath Does (Free Version)

  • Set breath cycle count (1–10 cycles)
  • Define inhale, hold, exhale, and post-exhale durations (1–10 seconds each)
  • Set a structured stillness period (1–10 minutes)
  • Use silent affirmations for each phase
  • Receive spoken phase cues and countdown guidance
  • Experience haptic alignment with each breath interval
  • Generate a deterministic geometric sigil based on your session inputs

✦ The Breath Structure

Inhale → Hold → Exhale → Hold
Repeated for your chosen number of cycles. After cycles complete, you enter a defined Stillness period.
When Stillness ends, the app renders a geometric sigil derived from:
  • Cycle count
  • Phase timing ratios
  • Stillness duration
Your breath becomes geometry.
