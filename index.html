✦ What Spiral Breath Does (Free Version)

Spiral Breath allows you to:

  • Set breath cycle count (1–10 cycles)
  • Define inhale, hold, exhale, and post-exhale durations (1–10 seconds each)
  • Set a structured stillness period (1–10 minutes)
  • Use silent affirmations for each phase
  • Receive spoken phase cues and countdown guidance
  • Experience haptic alignment with each breath interval
  • Generate a deterministic geometric sigil based on your session inputs

Every session becomes a structured harmonic construct.

✦ The Breath Structure

Each session follows a four-phase cycle:

Inhale → Hold → Exhale → Hold

Repeated for your chosen number of cycles. After the cycles complete, you enter a defined Stillness period.

When Stillness ends, the app renders a geometric sigil derived from:

  • Cycle count
  • Phase timing ratios
  • Stillness duration

Your breath becomes geometry.

✦ Why Structure Matters

Spiral Breath does not randomize breath. It creates:

  • Deterministic timing
  • Measured countdown pacing
  • Phase integrity
  • Controlled stillness

The voice guidance names each phase. The countdown stabilizes rhythm. Haptic feedback anchors embodiment. Breath becomes deliberate.

✦ Silent Affirmations

Each phase supports customizable silent affirmations:

  • Inhale affirmation
  • Hold affirmation
  • Exhale affirmation
  • Hold affirmation
  • Stillness affirmation

These are displayed during the session but not spoken. You choose the language. The structure holds it.

✦ Deterministic Sigil Output

At session completion, Spiral Breath generates a geometric sigil.

The sigil is calculated from:

  • Total cycles
  • Relative phase proportions
  • Stillness normalization
  • Prime-cycle harmonic drift
  • Golden angle recursion

The output is deterministic. Identical inputs produce identical sigils. Your breath session leaves a geometric trace — a symbolic encoding of the structure you enacted.
